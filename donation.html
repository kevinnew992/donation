Rambuke roots Reforestation Initiative

Help Us Protect Sri Lanka’s Vanishing Plants

1 Million Plants by 2050

Many native and endangered plant species in Sri Lanka are on the brink of disappearing forever. Through this long‑term program, Rambuke roots is committed to planting 1,000,000 carefully selected native and threatened plants across Sri Lanka by 2050.

Your contribution directly funds nursery development, sapling production, farmer partnerships, and monitored planting in sensitive ecosystems. Every donation – large or small – helps restore biodiversity and support rural communities.

Transparent, Impact‑Driven Giving

Each contribution is tracked against our 1M plants by 2050 target with regular impact updates.
